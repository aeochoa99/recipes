Salsa Verde

Description

A quick and easy salsa verde recipe that is perfect for street tacos and requires only a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients

  1. 4 tomatillos
  2. 1 jalapeño
  3. 2 serranos
  4. 1 handful of cilantro
  5. 2 garlic cloves
  6. 1 chicken bouillon cube
  7. 1tbsp of black pepper
  8. 1tbsp of salt
