Salsa Verde

Description

A quick and easy salsa verde recipe that is perfect for street tacos and requires only a handful of ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 tomatillos
  • 1 jalapeño
  • 2 serranos
  • 1 handful of cilantro
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 chicken bouillon cube
  • 1tbsp of black pepper
  • 1tbsp of salt
  • 1 cup of water

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil.
  2. Boil the tomatillos until their color changes to a darker dull green, then remove and place into a blender.
  3. Next boil the jalapeño and serranos until they soften slightly, cut off the stems and add the softened peppers to the blender.
  4. Place the 2 garlic gloves and cilantro to the blender.
  5. Add 1 cup of water to the blender. (You can use some of the water you boiled.)
  6. Add the chicken bouillon, salt, and pepper to the blender.
  7. Blend to your desired consistency.
