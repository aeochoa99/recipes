Smoked Salmon Sandwich

Description

A quick and easy sandwich packed full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of whole wheat bread
  • 1tbsp cream cheese
  • 1 tbsp creamy goat cheese
  • 2 slices smoked salmon
  • 1tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp dried dill
  • Ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. Toast bread in a toaster or toaster oven.
  2. Spread cream cheese and goat cheese on 1 slice of toast. Add smoked salmon on top. Add olive oil, dill, and black pepper. Cover with lettuce and top with the other slice of toast.
