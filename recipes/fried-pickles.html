Fried Pickles

Description

Air fried crispy and golden dill pickles that are a perfect appetizer for any gathering.

Ingredients

  • 20 dill cornichons, patted dry
  • 1 cup all purpose flour
  • 3 large egg whites
  • 2tbsp water
  • 2/3 cup of breadcrumbs
  • 2/3 cup yellow cornmeal
  • 2tbsp seasoning salt
  • Avocado cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat an airfryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C). Drain cornichons and pat dry with paper towels.
  2. Add flour to a shallow bowl. Whisk egg whites with water in another shallow bowl. In a third bowl whisk together breadcrumbs, cornmeal, and seasoning salt.
  3. Toss 3 to 4 pickles at a time into flour. Shake off any excess. Dip into egg wash; shake off any excess, then roll in breadcrumb mixture. Carefully press breadcrumbs onto each pickle. Place onto a baking sheet or plate. Spray with an even coat of cooking spray. Repeat with remaining pickles.
  4. Transfer half the pickles into the airfryer and spray them again with cooking spray if necessary. Cook the pickles for 6 to 8 minutes. Repeat with remaining pickles. Place them onto a paper towel lined plate.
